# Contributing Guidelines

Thank you for your interest in contributing!

## How to Contribute

1. Fork the repository.
2. Create a new branch for your changes.
3. Add or edit protocol documents in the relevant folder.
4. Open a Pull Request with a clear description of your changes.

## Proposals

- For major protocol changes, open an Issue first to discuss your idea.
- Please follow the structure and style of existing documents.

## Review Process

- All contributions will be reviewed by maintainers.
- Feedback may be provided, and changes may be requested before merging.

## Code of Conduct

By participating, you agree to abide by our [Code of Conduct](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).
